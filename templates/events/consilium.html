Team size: 1 - 2

Description

Consilium is a Digital Electronics based event where the students will design complex digital systems and simulate them on Proteus. The problem statement will be chosen from real-life examples that can be synthesised using digital electronics concepts.

Problem Statement 1 Register

Workshops

  1. Number System and Boolean Algebra

    Analog and digital signals, Number system, Boolean algebra, Logic gates and Karnaugh maps

  2. Combinational Circuits

    Binary Number Representation-Unsigned magnitude representation, Signed magnitude Representation, 1's complement representation, 2's complement representation; Combinational Circuits-Adders, Decoder, Encoder, Multiplexer, Demultiplexer.

  3. Intro to Proteus and Sequential Circuits

    SR latch using NOR and NAND, Gated SR latch, Introduction to flips flops and Proteus simulations

  4. Types of Flip Flops

    SR flip flop, D flip flop, JK flip flop, T flip flop and conversion of one flip flop to other

  5. Finite State Machine

    Finite State Machines, Mealy and Moore State Machine, State reduction and Counters
